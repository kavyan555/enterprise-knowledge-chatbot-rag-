Company Policy - ABC Corporation

Working Hours:
Employees are expected to work 9 hours per day, including breaks.
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

Code of Conduct:
All employees must maintain professionalism, respect colleagues, and follow ethical standards.

Remote Work:
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Security Policy:
Sharing confidential company data outside the organization is strictly prohibited.

IT Policy:
Employees must not install unauthorized software on company systems.
